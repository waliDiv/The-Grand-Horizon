Our Story

Since 1925, The Grand Horizon has been a beacon of luxury and timeless elegance. Located in the heart of the city, we've welcomed guests from around the world.

Our hotel was founded by the Vanderbilt family with a vision to create a sanctuary of comfort and style. Over the decades, we've preserved our historic charm while continuously modernizing to exceed expectations.

Today, we remain a family-owned property dedicated to personalized service, exquisite design, and culinary excellence.
